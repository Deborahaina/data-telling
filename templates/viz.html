Trending Stocks

Stocks are said to be trending based on different factors.It depends on who you are asking.Stocks can be trending as a result of price action, social media buzz, news articles, trade volume and purely by techincal analysis.

It is often difficult to make an informed decision based on social media or news frenzy but time and time again we have seen short term stock trend as a result of news articles. It is always advisable to make your decisions based on the data available to you.

The most widely-used techincal analysis used is the 200-day moving average. The perception is that, if a stock continues to hold above or go higher than the 200-day moving average, it is in a long-term uptrend. This plot displays daily trending stocks from yahoo finance.
