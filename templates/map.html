Rodent Patrol

New York City's non-emergency line (311) is for service requests. One can call in and report anything from noise complaint, blocked driveway, noncompliance with phased reopening (COVID19), rodents, illegal parking each day. This is handled by the New York city police department. Here she will be getting her hands dirty by mapping 311 service requests reporting rodents. New York City has been dealing with the issue of rodent infestattion and she would like to help the city council determine where they may be to facilitate a plan of action. The initial call returns the number of rodents (sightings, signs of rodents, condition attracting rodents etc..) Click on the number to discover more information. Leaflet has a Marker cluster plugin which helps to declutter a map with tons of data on it.

The data is sourced from https://data.cityofnewyork.us/Social-Services/311-Service-Requests-from-2010-to-Present/erm2-nwe9/data"
