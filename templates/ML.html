Machine Learning

I explore the use of various machine learning algorithms to predict, classifiy and deduce dimensionality in datatsets. Machine learning is critical now because of the enormous amount of data we collect everyday and simply guessing what the hidden insights in the data will not be the most effective way to use it. Machine learning makes it possible for us to train a model using the datasets available and then using that model to predict unknown data outside of the trained set. Depending on the kind of data(contiunous, discrete, categorical), we will have to choose the appropriate alogorithms to use.
